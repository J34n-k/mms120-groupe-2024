Dating Disruption: How Online Dating Apps(Bumble, Tinder) Impact Traditional In-Person Dating

Disruptive technologies continue to reshape various aspects of society, challenging conventional norms and practices. Online dating apps like Bumble and Tinder exemplify this innovation by redefining how people connect, moving away from traditional courtship and in-person matchmaking. These platforms offer a digital alternative that makes dating more accessible while raising questions about authenticity, depth, and societal expectations around love. In this website, we will delve into how online dating acts as a disruptive force, reshaping romantic relationships and its impacts on society’s view of love.
